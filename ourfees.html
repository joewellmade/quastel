Home Contents Appraisals

Appraisal fees are calculated on the size of your home, using bedroom number as a guide. We use the estate agent’s description; to mean any room normally classed as a bedroom, regardless of current use.

1–2 Bedrooms £390
3–5 bedrooms £440
6–8 Bedrooms £490
Each additional bedroom £50

Bespoke Valuations

Detailed valuations of individual items or home contents for insurance, inheritance tax or family division. Our standard call out fee of three hours is applicable when valuing four or more items.

1–3 Items £340
Three Hours £590
Per Hour Thereafter £140

Jewellery valuations
